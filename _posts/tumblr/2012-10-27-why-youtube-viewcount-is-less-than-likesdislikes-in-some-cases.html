--- tags: - youtube title: Why youtube viewcount is less than likes/dislikes in some cases layout: post ---

You must have seen a lot of times that the user viewcount is less compared to the number of likes/dislikes for a video. Like in the picture below.

.                                 

It isn’t some kind of issue with youtube. 

For the first few hundred views, they increment in realtime. Beyond that viewcounts update, at slowest, every few hours and often more quickly, but not in realtime. This has to do with their methodology for ensuring that spam/invalid views are not counted since they take view count seriously.
