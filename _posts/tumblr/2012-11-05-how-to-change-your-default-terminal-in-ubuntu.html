--- tags: title: How to change your default terminal in ubuntu layout: post ---

You may be shifting  from the default gnome terminal to  a new terminal like terminator. You may find this line handy.

gconftool --type string --set /desktop/gnome/applications/terminal/exec YOUR-TERMINAL-NAME

In this case it is

gconftool --type string --set /desktop/gnome/applications/terminal/exec terminator
